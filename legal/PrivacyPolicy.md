# Unity Tunnel VPN - Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** [Date]

Unity Tunnel VPN ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and what we do NOT collect when you use our free, ad-supported VPN application.

## 1. What We Do NOT Collect (No-Logs Policy for Traffic)
We are a privacy-first utility. We do **NOT** monitor, record, log, store, or pass to any third party:
- Your browsing history or visited websites.
- Your internet traffic destinations or data content.
- Your originating IP address beyond the active session connection.
- Your DNS queries.

## 2. What We Do Collect
To operate the service, manage the balance system, and support the app through advertising, we collect:
- **Connection Timestamps & Duration:** To enforce the 15-minute free tier and rewarded time balances.
- **Data Transfer Volume:** To enforce bandwidth limits (if applicable) and prevent abuse.
- **Ad Interaction Data:** We record when you watch a rewarded video to credit your account securely.
- **Crash Logs & Analytics:** Anonymous diagnostic data (e.g., app crashes, device OS version) to improve stability.

## 3. Third-Party Advertising
This is a free, ad-supported application. We use third-party mediation SDKs (such as AppLovin MAX) to serve advertisements. These partners may collect device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) and approximate location to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your Android system settings.

## 4. User Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to request deletion of your account/balance data. Since we do not require an account to use the service, simply uninstalling the application and clearing app data will remove your local identifiers.
